Hummus

Everyone loves a good hummus with bread or crackers

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Quality Waterfilter
  • Rosemary
  • One Lemon
  • One garlic clove
  • 1 tsp sea salt

Steps

  1. Place the dried Chickpeas in a pot that is double the size of the chickpeas that you use
  2. Add filtered water, at least 2x the amount of what you used in grams for the chickpeas
  3. Let it sit for 12hours at least
  4. Cook it up and let it stir for 3 hours
  5. Place the hummus in a blender for 3 minutes
  6. Slice the garlic and the lemon
  7. Add it to the hummus
  8. Add one tsp of salt
  9. Done.
